3D Knitting



I would like to teach the 3D printer to knit.

Knitting on a 3D printer.

I am fashinated by this beautiful technique of one thread constructing a beautifull flexible fabric.

Can I construct a flexible fabric with the 3D printer? Since I started this project, I discovered that the main difference with knitting is that the 3D printer can't put the thread through a loop of a thread. The loops are laying on top of each other, but on the other hand my 3D printer is creating a flexible fabric with one thread of filement.

Challenges

  • A smooth wire
  • How much filement does the printer extrude between two points.
  • How thin can I print
  • How fast can I print
  • Difference between a knit and a purl stitch.
